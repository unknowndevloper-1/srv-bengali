About SRV Open Labs

Dedicated to creating modern software solutions and restoring digital heritage.
Our primary mission is simple but vital: making regional keyboards better.

Our Heritage

Our journey is rooted in the legacy of the SRV Web Development & Software Development Team, which began around 2008 under the leadership of Sourav Maiti & Bidisa Pradhan. While the original team has moved on to other ventures, the spirit of their work lives on.

The New Era

Founded in 2026, SRV Open Labs is a distinct, independent organization committed to continuing that original legacy. We are an Open Source project currently focused on:

  • Modernizing old SRV ANSI Keyboard layouts & Fonts for Unicode.
  • Restoring and preserving old SRV ANSI Keyboard layouts and Fonts.

Join the Mission

We are currently a small, passionate team. While we work hard to bridge the gap between old and new technologies, our size means progress can sometimes take time. We appreciate your patience as you use our current Unicode layout.
